After graduating from Hogwarts, Harry, Ron, and Hermione ended up becoming software developers for Gamma.

Why Us

The Data Services School of Witchcraft and Development for Gamma Ball Rally 2016

Team Transition!

There are some team structure changes happening within Data Services, and any opportunity to build our team relationship will help. A happy team is a productive team!

Development Exposure!

No, not related to the removal of wizard robes, but an opportunity for some of the more socially acceptable developers to finally meet our customers. We are a part of the business which is often locked away (not in a basement, at least), and any human client interaction is rare.

Sun and Fresh Air

For health reasons, we need to be given a reason to turn off our PCs, go outdoors and absorb vitamin D. A chance to practice our language skills too (because Java, PHP and SQL don't count).

Secret Weapon

LegacyJack knows how to change a tyre and where to find the engine. This knowledge will give us an advantage and practically guarantee victory.
